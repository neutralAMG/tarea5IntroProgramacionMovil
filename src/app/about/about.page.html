About the Series

The Stormlight Archive is an epic fantasy book series written by Brandon Sanderson. It is planned to have **ten books** split into two arcs of five. The series is part of Sanderson's Cosmere universe.

Books Released So Far

 The Way of Kings (2010) Words of Radiance (2014) Oathbringer (2017) Rhythm of War (2020) Wind and truth (2024)

Genres & Publisher

Genres: Epic Fantasy, High Fantasy, Magic Systems

Publisher: Tor Books
